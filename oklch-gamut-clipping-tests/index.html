OkLCH gamut clipping tests

Tests with two methods of out of gamut colors back into P3.

When using an oklch() color with a chroma out of the screen gamut, at time of writing, most browsers will choose the closest color by clipping the individual RGB value of the color.

The problem is that the resulting color will have a lightness and hue shift, more or less important depending on the chroma value, which is a bummer since it defeat the OkLCH uniform advantage.

That means that by reading the color code, e.g. oklch(80% 0.37 20), you might think that you'll get a color with a lightness of 80%, where in fact it'll be 65% in that case. A better approach is to reduce only the chroma until the color is in the targeted gamut.
